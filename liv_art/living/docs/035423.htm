Published Friday, May 10, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

LANCOME IS LAUNCHING A NEW FRAGRANCE, ``POEME,'' WITH A LOVE POETRY WRITING CONTEST. THE CHILDREN'S LITERACY ORGANIZATION READING IS FUNDAMENTAL WILL BENEFIT. SO WILL THE GRAND PRIZE WINNER, WHO GETS A TRIP TO FRANCE.

The competition is divided into three age categories: 7-11, 12-17, 18 and older. Besides the trip for two to Paris in the adult category and $10,000 savings bonds in the other two categories, computers and poetry books will be awarded. Deadline for entries is June 7. Entry blanks are available by calling (800) 797-POEM or at Lancome counters.

  • Looking good: Christine Sherlock, co-author of Color Me Beautiful's Looking Your Best (Madison Books, $14.95), will appear Tuesday at the Hyatt Regency Pier 66, 2301 SE 17th Causeway, Fort Lauderdale. She'll hold a free seminar from 7:30 to 9 p.m. on dressing and making up to accentuate your best features. For reservations, call (800) COLOR ME.
