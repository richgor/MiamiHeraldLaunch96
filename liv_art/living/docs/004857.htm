NEW AND NOTEWORTHY BOOKS

  • Storm Chaser: In Pursuit of Untamed Skies, by Warren Faidley. A thrilling first-person account of one man's often successful attempts to photograph tornadoes and thunderstorms from close range. It has just been published by the Weather Channel. To order, call (800) 544-6206.

  • Weather, a primer by the Nature Co. This beautifully illustrated layman's guide explains everything from how to read a weather map to the nature of El Niño. The book, released only days ago, was prepared by top meteorologists and climatologists. To order, call (800) 277-8844.

  • Encyclopedia of Climate and Weather, a two-volume set released by Oxford University Press. A highly detailed primer written in layman's terms. To order, call (800) 451-7556.

    CD-ROM

  • Everything Weather, an interactive weather primer from the Weather Channel that contains video and photos of tornadoes, hurricanes and floods, a 480-weather glossary, and a climate database for 500 U.S. cities and 200 cities abroad. If you have a modem, you can use the CD-ROM to get the latest weather in 200 cities. To order, call (800) 633-2222.

  • Operation: Weather Disaster, an interactive game from the Discovery Channel that challenges users to stop an evil weatherman from inflicting deadly and destructive storms on earth. To order, call (800) 762-2189.
