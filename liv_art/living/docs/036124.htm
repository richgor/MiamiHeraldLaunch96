  • PET LOSS SUPPORT HOTLINE: THE UNIVERSITY OF FLORIDA IN GAINESVILLE OFFERS PET GRIEVING COUNSELING BY VETERINARY STUDENT VOLUNTEERS, STAFFED WEEKDAYS FROM 7 TO 9 P.M. CALL (904) 392-4700, THEN DIAL 1 AND 4080.

  • Pet Loss Support Group: The Humane Society of Greater Miami sponsors a pet loss support group that meets the first and third Tuesdays of each month at the Kendall branch of the Dade Public Library, 9101 SW 97th Ave., from 7:30 p.m. to 9 p.m. Information: (305) 696-0800 between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m. Mondays-Saturdays.
