Published Friday, April 26, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

YOU GO

  • The Seventh Annual Latin Music Conference & Awards Shows, Monday-Wednesday at the Hotel Inter-Continental, 100 Chopin Plaza, Miami, includes panels on contract negotiations, an overview of Tejano music and a writers-in-the-round session as well as showcases and a closing awards show.

  • Billboard's International Latin Music Awards, featuring live performances by Albita, Heroes del Silencio, Pete Astudillo and Jose Feliciano, takes place at the Gusman Center for the Performing Arts, 174 E. Flagler St., Miami, at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday. Balcony tickets are still available at $20.

    Awards, based on sales, will be given in the Pop, Tropical and Regional Mexican categories as well as video, jazz and dance. Gloria Estefan will collect a Spirit of Hope Award for her humanitarian efforts, Feliciano will receive a Lifetime Achievement Award and Juan Gabriel will be inducted into the Hall of Fame.

  • All events except the awards show are for registered participants only. Registration costs $350. For more information call (212) 536-5002 or (305) 441-7976.
