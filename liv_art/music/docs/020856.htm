MISSION ACCOMPLISHED

An early rollout of publicity for this summer's Tom Cruise vehicle, Mission: Impossible has begun in an unlikely place: Clubs. The familiar Lalo Shifrin theme from the '60s TV series has been revamped by U2's Adam Clayton and Larry Mullen. The tune keeps its air of suspense, yet dance beats modernize it. Hot remixer/DJ Junior Vasquez produced an up-tempo house version (Mission Accomplished) and the tune is considered Billboard's No. 1 ``dance breakout'' in club play this week.
