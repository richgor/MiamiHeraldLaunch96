Published Friday, April 26, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

  • Felicia Michele Haber's weekly guide to upcoming events in Broward and North Dade isn't meant to be all-inclusive. For

    • more details, see listings starting this page. Friday: Happening happy hour at Parrot Lounge; wear your favorite Levi's; (local) crowds after 9 p.m. Travel the world while sipping imported beers at Billabong's in Hallandale; pool tables; jukebox; leave attitude behind. Need a no-brainer? Relax at the Elbo Room in Fort Lauderdale; chat with tourists; drink beer; balcony has great people-watching view.

    Saturday: Stock up on suntan oil at T-Zers II in Pompano Beach; grand-opening party; free T-shirts and suntan lotion to first 200 people; Bud Lite girls; free dolphin buffet. Hopelessly romantic types, head to Pier 66's rooftop lounge; revolving bar; live music; panoramic view of Fort Lauderdale. Party at O'Hara's; live jazz and crowds after 9 p.m.; good cappuccino next door at Cafe Europa.

    Sunday: Take your kinky thoughts to Squeeze; the Eroticville Theatre premieres; sexy magicians; Burlesque cabaret; leather-clad strippers (leave the kids home). Tie dye and braids are the theme in Hallandale; reggae party at Cafe Casablanca; Jamaican scene. Bid the weekend adios at Shooters in North Miami Beach; indoor bar with Intracoastal view.

    Monday: Melrose Place action at Pickles Nite Spot in Pompano Beach; rehash story lines until 4 a.m.; late night drink specials start at midnight. Amateur strip contest at The Copa; Tiny Tina hosts; no cover; starts 1:30 a.m. Mellow out at Thunder Alley in North Miami Beach; watch sports on monitors; play pool; check out docked sports boats.

    Tuesday: If you're pinching pennies, head to Tugboat Annie's; drafts are 75 cents; crowd is laid-back; water view. If your Versace slacks are in the cleaners, try Riptide Sports Bar in Fort Lauderdale; blue jeans rule; pool tables; video games. Eat and drinking fest at East Side Mario's in North Miami Beach; cheap wings and beer; general snob alert; dig out your name brands.

    Wednesday: Toss out the frozen drink mix; drop by Cielito Lindo's midweek margarita party (4480 N. Federal Hwy., Lighthouse Point); margaritas 99 cents; chips and salsa; crowd peaks after 10 p.m. Cheap thrills at the Chickee Bar in Fort Lauderdale; male and female revue at 9 p.m.; sorry, only ladies drink free. Find those stretch pants and stuff your face with all-you-can-eat spaghetti dinner at Antics Grill in Cooper City. If you want to talk, it's also karaoke night.

    Thursday: Testosterone has its privileges at T-Zers II; men's night starts at 10 p.m.; two-for-one drinks for men only. If you're on a roll -- rock 'n' roll that is -- head to Supersports Rock Lounge in Lauderhill; live music; darts; bowling. Ladies Night at Biz Bistro (19575 Biscayne Blvd.), Aventura Mall; free appetizers; ladies get discounted drinks; get jazzed up for a weekend at Cheers; live jazz and blues; show up after 9:30 p.m.

    Fax or call Felicia Michele Haber with club news for consideration in her Party Planner at (954) 961-6727.
