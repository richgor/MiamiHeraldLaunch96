These gifts have `Mom' written all over them, just in time

Check your calendar. Mother's Day is Sunday, so avoid guilt later -- buy now.

Mom always said . . .

Lay it on thick with mother nature, a gift collection that includes a spray cologne, hand cream and a book titled the philosophy of love. Heavy on stylish packaging and lower case letters, each item has a philosophy-lite message printed on it.

The fragrance is called ``grace.'' On its package: ``how you climb up the mountain is just as important as how you get down the mountain . . . ''

And the box that everything comes in: ``a mother loves us in a way that only nature understands. like nature, she is forever giving, asking for nothing in return.''

Go ahead and gag but, look, Mom's got tears in her eyes!

Price: $66.

Available: (888) 263-9243.
Hot, humid, but hip
We're so cool -- South Florida, that is. Put our name on merchandise and, hey, we'll bite. The latest in local cachet ranges from $5-a-bottle nail lacquer to -- yikes -- $2,500 jeans. Oh, but they've got 14K gold buttons with palm trees.

Can't spare the two-plus grand? The sterling silver version of Denimwear by Dorethea of Palm Beach goes for $500.

Don't despair if you've got a single-digit budget. If we believe In Style magazine, Sharon Stone's makeup artist gives a thumbs up to OPI's Caribbean Collection of oh-so-cleverly named nail lacquers. Among the 12 hues are How to Jamaica Million (pearl) and Ponce de Lilac (lavender).

Also, for local flavor, we have: Moon Over Miami (pale blue), South Beach Blush (pink) and Florida Key Lime Pie.

Jeans: Raymonde G. in Palm Beach, (407) 833-9526.

Nail lacquer: Call (800) 341-9999 for store nearest you.
Hidden treasure

Mom's Treasure Chest, a small wooden box featuring an image of a mother and child, holds mementos, jewelry or keepsakes. Here's a nice touch. Inside each box is a card that tells Mom a donation ($8.20) is being made in her honor to help homeless moms and kids.

Created by The Lane Co., the box measures 9 by 4 1/2 by 3 1/2 inches.

Price: $29.95, includes shipping and handling.

Available: (800) 841-5147.

Power tripping mom

Forget the flowers, candy and froufrou for your down-to-business mom. Send her packing with The Stuffed Shirt case, which is laptop size and designed to hold a blouse, scarf, hosiery, undies and jewelry. A case for toiletries the size of a paperback attaches to the case, and it's small enough to slip into a slim attache.

Price: From $75 to $180, depending on the material.

Available: (800) 788-3333.

Picture perfect

The new twist on a favored tradition: Don't bronze those baby shoes, preserve them with a clear finish. Footnotes in Time covers shoes with a high-gloss clear finish, then mounts shoes on a beveled Lucite base alongside a personalized engraved brass plate.

Price: $59.95.

Available: (800) 772-8626.
