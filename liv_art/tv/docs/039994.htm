Published Tuesday, April 23, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Channel 7 announces changes


WSVN-Channel 7 is rearranging anchor chairs.

On Monday, Kelley Mitchell left her Deco Drive co-host spot to return to her anchor seat on the 5 p.m. news with Rick Sanchez. Tim Sherno takes her seat at the Deco Drive dais. Also, Marilyn Mitzel leaves the 5:30 p.m. anchor chair and returns to medical reporting. The 5:30 p.m. news is now anchored by Pam Giganti and Lynn Martinez.

According to Channel 7 News Director Alice Jacobs, the switch will allow Mitzel to ``devote more time to her Healthcast and health reporting.''

As for Mitchell, ``Kelley's been working hours that for any normal human would be impossible. We decided it was best for her to go back to a normal work schedule.'' The decision for Mitchell to drop Deco Drive in favor of the news, says Jacobs, ``was mutual.''
