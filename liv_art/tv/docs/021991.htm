Tune in for another sweeps month


By ROBIN DOUGHERTY
Herald Television Critic

Cliffhangers, weddings and babies: Sweeps month runs from now through the end of May. It's one of four periods each year that determine what television can charge for advertising time.

What follows are the highlights of sweeps-month special programming -- most shamelessly designed to lure you in or send you screaming -- as well as season finales for many series. (Although PBS and cable networks don't participate in this sweeps, we've included notable programming anyway.)

Programs are identified by network. The South Florida stations for each are: PBS, channels 2 and 42; ABC, channels 10 and 25; CBS, channels 4 and 12; NBC, channels 6 and 5; Fox, channels 7 and 29; WB, channel 39; and UPN, channels 33 and 34.

Newsradio (NBC) 8:30 p.m. -- Season finale.

Grumpy Old Men (CBS) 9 p.m. -- Jack Lemmon and Walter Matthau as two old men in love with Ann-Margret.

All-New All-Star TV Censored Mega-Bloopers (NBC) 8 p.m. -- Oops!

The People Next Door (CBS) 9 p.m. -- Faye Dunaway, Nicollette Sheridan and Michael O'Keefe star in a thriller about a woman whose neighbors are creepy.
