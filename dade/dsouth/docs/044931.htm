Bethel Baptist in Richmond Heights will host Church Ministry Summit

Bishop Noel Jones of Greater Bethany Community Church in Los Angeles, and Don Meares, Ph.D., will lead a Church Ministry Summit from May 17-19 at the Bethel Baptist Church, 14440 Lincoln Blvd., Richmond Heights.

Jones wrote his first book, Church development, after completing a Bachelor of Theology degree at Aenon Bible College. He currently teaches Bible classes that are broadcast throughout the Los Angeles community. He will speak at 7:30 p.m. May 17.

Meares, senior pastor of Evangel Church in Washington, D.C., wrote Corporate Destiny in the Local Church. He is host of the National Pastor's Conference each March in his home. He will conduct a workshop on Local Church Leadership and Unity in the Body of Christ at 10 a.m. May 18. Services May 19 will be at 7:30 and 9 a.m. and 5:30 p.m.

The New Birth Recording Mass Choir will be the special musical guest at the Sunday workshop. A $10 registration fee is required. Call 235-7423.
