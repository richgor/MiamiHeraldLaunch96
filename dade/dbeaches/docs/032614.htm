Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Rabbis honored at annual dinner

The annual dinner for Yeshiva Elementary School and Mesivta of Greater Miami will be May 26.

The dinner begins at 6 p.m. at the Crown Hotel, 4041 Collins Ave. This year's honorees include: Rabbi Zevulon Glixman, Rabbi Yaacov Orimland, and Marcos and Tiffany Lapciuc.

At the dinner, Glixman will receive the Rabbinical Leadership Award in recognition of the Jewish education and guidance he has provided to young people.

Orimland will be awarded the Mechanech Award as recognition for his 15 years of dedication to Jewish education in Miami Beach.

In addition, the Parents of the Year award will go to the Lapciucs, who have been active in fund-raising efforts for the Talmudic University of Florida.

The community is invited to attend. For more information or to make reservations, call 538-5543.
