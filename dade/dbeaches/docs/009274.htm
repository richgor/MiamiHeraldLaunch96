Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Money stolen while woman took shower

A burglar stole $250 out of a woman's purse while she was taking a shower at her apartment in the 9200 block of Collins Avenue in Surfside between 9:30 and 9:45 p.m. May 1. When the woman got out of the shower, she noticed her purse had been moved to the couch. When she check, she found the money was missing from her wallet. A watch and some jewelry were also taken off of the night stand.


MIAMI BEACH

BURGLARS BROKE into a room at the Clay Hotel, 1530 Washington Ave., between 8 and 10 p.m. Thursday. The suspects ransacked the room before stealing $100.

POLICE SUSPECT a worker at the Eden Roc Hotel, 4525 Collins Ave., stole $75 worth of shrimp, chicken and lobster from the hotel between 8:50 and 9 p.m. Thursday.

A MAN WAS ROBBED at gunpoint in the hallway of Temple Kneseth Israel, 1415 Euclid Ave., at 2:50 p.m. Thursday. The gunman made the man lie on the floor and took his wallet from his jacket and his watch from his wrist.

THIEVES STOLE a $160 watch and $200 from a towel in the 700 block of the beach at around 6 p.m. Wednesday while their owner went to buy a soda.

SURFSIDE
