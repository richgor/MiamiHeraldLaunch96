Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

USE THIS ONE

The Humane Society of Greater Miami's main shelter is at 2101 NW 95th St. The phone is 696-0800. Here are its operating hours:

  • Adoptions: 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Monday through Friday; 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday.

  • Animal drop-off: 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. Monday through Friday; 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Saturday.

  • Well-animal clinic, for vaccinations and sterilization: 1 to 3 p.m. Tuesday through Friday; 7:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Saturday.

    The Cutler Ridge shelter, which has more limited services, is at 19151 S. Dixie Hwy. The phone is 252-3389. Here are its hours:

  • Adoptions: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Saturday, with a break from 2 to 4 p.m. Monday, Wednesday and Friday.

  • Animal drop-off: 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday.

  • Well-animal clinic: 2 to 4 p.m. Monday, Wednesday and Friday.

    To donate cash, send a check to the Humane Society of Greater Miami, 2101 NW 95th St., Miami, Fla. 33147.

    To donate time, call the volunteer coordinator at 696-0800.
