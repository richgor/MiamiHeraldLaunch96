Magical Mother'sDay
New arrivals make it a
momentous for new moms

Today, amid cards, balloons and flowers, Sue McGee is celebrating Mother's Day with newborn daugher Kelly, who also meets the approval of older sisters Christie, 7, and Shannon, 9.

Throughout South Florida, scores of moms, both new and veterans, are spending Mother's Day rejoicing and recuperating from childbirth. Maybe that is why Mother's Day comes in May: It's the start of the baby season.
