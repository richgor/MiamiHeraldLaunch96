Published Saturday, May 11, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

MUSIC AWARDS

Gloria Estefan was a four-award winner in Univision's Lo Nuestro Awards presentation Thursday at the James L. Knight Center. Enrique Iglesias, singer Julio Iglesias' son, picked up three awards. The honorees in the Spanish-language TV network's annual fest are:
POP/BALLAD

  • Album: Enrique Iglesias, by Enrique Iglesias.

  • Male Artist: Luis Miguel.

  • Female Artist: Gloria Estefan.

  • Group: Marco Antonio Solis and Los Bukis.

  • Newcomer: Enrique Iglesias

  • Song: Si tu te vas, by Enrique Iglesias.
    TROPICAL/SALSA

  • Album: Abriendo Puertas, by Gloria Estefan.

  • Male Artist: Marc Anthony.

  • Female Artist: Gloria Estefan.

  • Group: Los Del Rio.

  • Newcomer: Ilegales.

  • Song: Abriendo Puertas, by Gloria Estefan.
    REGIONAL/MEXICAN

  • Album: El Mexico que se nos fue, by Juan Gabriel.

  • Male Artist: Cristian Castro.

  • Female Artist: Ana Barbara.

  • Group: La Mafia and Marco Antonio Solis and Los Bukis.

  • Newcomer: Pete Astudillo.

  • Song: Como te extraño, by Pete Astudillo.

    Musical video:

  • Video: Te extraño, te olvido, te amo, by Ricky Martin.
