Published Tuesday, May 7, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

TAKING A SAMPLE WARREN ZINN / For The Herald

JEFFERY A. SALTER / For The Herald
Police can now use DNA samples to find missing children. A kit that extracts DNA from cheek cells on a cotton swab was created by former Miami Beach Detective Sgt. Joe Matthews. Miami Beach Police demonstrate the kit on Miami Beach elementary students. Lorenzo Hernandez, 8, of South Pointe Elementary School, has a DNA sample taken, which will be kept by his parents. Daniel Lind, Jason Lewis and Julie Ruiz await their turn. The Child Trace Home DNA Identification Kit, left, will be given to all Miami Beach elementary school students. Story, 3B.
