ALDO GONZALEZ Miami Beach Math comes naturally to Aldo Gonzalez, and so does helping those for whom math or computers does not come so naturally.

Gonzalez was frustrated to see fellow students -- including his sister -- struggle with the math section of the High School Competency Test.

Using programming he learned interning at NASA, Gonzalez created a series of tests that encouraged students to participate in after-school study sessions for the exam.

``The students have had such a positive reaction,'' said math chair Joan Friedman. ``Occasionally, a class is enriched by such a student: a student who forces others to think, who puts himself, not his teachers, in charge of learning, and who cares about others.''

Gonzalez ranks third in his class and scored 1480 on the SAT, including a perfect 800 on the math portion.
