Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Brother Jose heading to CBS

Herald Staff Report

Jose Diaz-Balart, noon and 5:30 p.m. anchor for Miami's NBC 6-WTVJ, is going national, co-anchoring a CBS morning show, WTVJ confirmed late Tuesday.

Diaz-Balart, a Fort Lauderdale native, will join a revamped CBS This Morning team anchored by Jane Robelot, news reader for the show, and rounded out with CBS This Morning meteorologist Mark McEwen, according to wire reports.
