Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Lawyer named county judge

Cecilia Maria Altonaga, an assistant Dade County attorney, has been appointed by Gov. Lawton Chiles to be a county court judge.

She replaces Judge Marc Shumacher, who was promoted to a circuit judgeship. The term expires in January.

Altonaga, 33, represented the county in commercial and construction litigation, drafted legislation for the county commission and participated in administrative hearings. She has been a member of the county's legal department since 1988. Before that, she clerked for U.S. District Judge Edward Davis and for the county attorney's office.

Altonaga is a Yale Law School graduate who received her bachelor's degree in history from Florida International University. A Coral Gables resident, she is married and the mother of two children.

She will take office June 14.
