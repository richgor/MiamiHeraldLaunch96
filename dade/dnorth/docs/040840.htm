Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Show off talents at Aventura Mall

Aventura Mall is hosting its second Talent Night on Wednesday.

At 5:30 p.m., local talents will hit center court in the mall, 19501 Biscayne Blvd., to sing, act and play instruments. With five acts signed up as of Friday, special events manager Laura Nichols said they have room for 20 more talents.

Each act can last up to three minutes. First, second and third place winners will receive a gift certificate good at any store in the mall.

Said Nichols: ``You can expect to be entertained.''
