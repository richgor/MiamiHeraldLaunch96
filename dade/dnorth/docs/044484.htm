Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Work of students, professor on display

The South Florida Art Center is sponsoring three exhibits this month. The ``Art Adventures Student Exhibition'' will feature the work of Miami Beach public school students and will run from Saturday until May 26.

It will be at the Ground Level Exhibition Center, 1035 Lincoln Road.

``Peace Islands'' will also recognize student artists and will be on display at both the Art 800, 800 Lincoln Rd., and at Common Space, 1655 Lenox Ave, all during May. ``CommonGround'' will feature the work of ceramic artist and University Miami professor Christine Federighi. It will be held at Clay Space, 1037 Lincoln Rd., from Saturday until July 26.

For more information call the South Florida Art Center at 674-8278.
