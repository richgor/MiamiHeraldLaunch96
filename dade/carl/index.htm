Preserving bay today is good sense for future
he most spectacular waters of Biscayne Bay are found in Biscayne National Park. This is no quirk of nature. An unpaved swath of coastline, the last in the county, is one reason the park has stayed so healthy. The Metro-Dade Commission has an extraordinary opportunity to keep it that way for your children and grandchildren, and perhaps generations to come.
