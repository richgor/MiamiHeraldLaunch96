Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Garages, patrols on agenda for meeting

This month's meeting of the Center Grove Neighborhood Association will discuss parking garages in the Village center, citizens on patrol and NET service requests. The meeting is at 7 p.m. May 13 at Hampton Inn Hotel behind Pollo Tropical on U.S. 1.

The annual membership donation of $15 is suggested and will be accepted at the door prior to the meeting. You may also mail or drop it off at 3290 Matilda St., Coconut Grove, Fla. 33133. For more information, call David Gell, association president, at 448-4884.

The Miami-Roads Neighborhood Civic Association and the Mary Brickell Garden Club are coordinating a series of environmental and historical tours for children from the Miramar and Southside elementary schools through June 11. They will include a tour of Simpson Park, an old-fashioned picnic at Brickell Park and a boat trip on the Miami River. The outings are sponsored by The Miami River Coordinating Committee, the Miami Parks Department, the First Presbyterian Church, the Miami police and fire departments and the Brickell Homeowners Association. For more information, call Pat Quintana, Simpson Park manager, 856-6801 10 a.m. to 5 p.m..

Miami-Roads elected officers and directors at the April meeting. Incumbent directors John Guinness, Dan Leibow and Carmen Petsoules were returned to office and will be joined by new directors Jose Sanchez and Alina Szmant. The following are the newly elected officers: Joe Wilkins president, John Guinness president-elect, Carmen Petsoules secretary/treasurer and Deborah Swain assistant secretary/treasurer. For suggestions or questions, call 858-1639.
The Westchester Home Owners' Association will hold its monthly meeting at 7:30 p.m. May 14 at Coral Park Elementary School, 1225 SW 97th Ave. The 24-hour hotline for WHOA is 410-9203.
