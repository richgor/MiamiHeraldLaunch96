Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Stay downtown for free concerts

Bayfront Park's After Dark Entertainment Series offers events every Thursday evening through May 30. All are free and start at 6:30 p.m. at the park's south end amphitheater stage at 301 N. Biscayne Blvd.

The schedule:

May 9: Freddick Bratcher & Company and Ballet Concerto Company.

May 16: Turner Technical High's 86 piece band.

May 23: Community Leaders in Concert. (They are looking for local politicians, judges, lawyers, chefs, etc.)

May 30: Eugene Neal (actor, dancer, singer and writer) will perform a one man theatrical show, The Conference of the Birds. He will be assisted by his son, Brian Neal, who is also a musician and composer.

Call 358-7550 for more information.
