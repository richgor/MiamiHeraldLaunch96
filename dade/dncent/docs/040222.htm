Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Speak your mind with commissioner

Commissioner Betty Ferguson is hosting a Town Hall meeting Tuesday evening to address concerns of residents in District 1.

Representatives from different county departments are scheduled to attend and answer questions.

``It is very important that we provide this kind of forum to ensure that the needs and desires of the community I represent are being met,'' Ferguson said. ``I encourage the public to attend the meeting and let their voices be heard.''

The meeting begins at 7 p.m. at Norland Elementary School, 19430 NW Eighth Ct.
-- MARIKA LYNCH
