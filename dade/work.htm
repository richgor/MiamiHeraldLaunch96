Tragedy on Campus
Friends, students mourn
murdered couple
Michael Postell, 22, holds a football given to him by his uncle, murdered University of Miami football star Marlin Barnes. The UM campus, and community at large, reacted with shock and grief to the brutal beating death on campus of Barnes and his girlfriend Timwanika Lumpkins, below. Lumpkins had received threats from a former boyfriend, police said.
