Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

NEIGHBORS IS LOOKING FOR ADULTS IN THEIR TWENTIES OR THIRTIES WHO AT ONE TIME ATTENDED THE AFTER SCHOOL HOUSE PROGRAM IN SOUTH MIAMI. WE ARE PLANNING TO WRITE A COVER STORY ABOUT FORMER STUDENTS WHO ATTENDED THE PROGRAM AND ITS EFFECT ON THEIR LIVES.

You do not have to live in South Miami to be included in the article.

Anyone who has any memories of the program is encouraged to call either Jennifer Gonzalez at 671-4356 or Pascale Etheart at 671-4351 any time.
