Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Silent, live auctions benefit Temple Shir Ami

Temple Shir Ami will have its annual May Madness Auction and Gourmet Adventure at 6:30 p.m. May 18 at the temple, 7205 SW 125th Ave.

The event features a dinner, a silent auction and a live auction that begins at 8:30 p.m. Items to be auctioned include: a 1985 BMW, original art, electronic equipment, jewelry, restaurant dinners, travel, airline tickets, weekends, sports collectibles and more.

Parties of 15 or more can purchase group tickets at $25 each if purchased by Monday. Regular admission is $30 per person if purchased before Saturday and $35 at the door. The auction is the temple's only big fund-raiser for the year. Call 279-7311.
