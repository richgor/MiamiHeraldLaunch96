Belen Jesuit Preparatory wins social studies bowl

The Social Studies Knowledge Bowl team from Belen Jesuit Preparatory won the County Championship at the 17th annual Dade County Social Studies Knowledge Bowl County Championship March 13. members are Jorge Fortun, Jose Ceide, Ivan Santa-Cruz, Jorge Sanchez, Pedro Carrillo, Arturo Balandra, and Ahmed Riesgo. Patrick Collins is department chairman and Jorge Martinez is coach. Jorge Sanchez is the team captain.
These are the South Miami Music Club scholarship winners for 1996, and their schools: First place, Jessica Victores, Florida Christian School; second place, Kelly Austin, Palmetto High, third place, Natalia Luckyanova, Ransom Everglades; fourth place, Christina Rosas-Guyon, Palmer Trinity; honorable mention: Audrey Siu, Palmetto High; Karly Kaplan, New World School of the Arts; Anthony Seepersad, School for the Advanced Studies; and Holli White, home school.
