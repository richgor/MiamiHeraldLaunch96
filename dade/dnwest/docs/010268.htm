Published Thursday, May 9, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

LOOKING FOR A DIFFERENT GIFT THIS MOTHER'S DAY? HERE ARE SOME IDEAS.

  • Tattoos by Lou's in Miami Beach offers a Mother's Day discount, with an average of $10 off most tattoos and body piercing. Call 532-7300.

  • Living Well Fitness Spa in Hialeah is offering $100 off their platinum, two-year membership. Call 556-5987.

  • Give mom a tourist's view of Miami. Go to Bayside, ride the Island Queen and catch glimpses of mansions belonging to Gloria Estefan, Julio Iglesias and Vanilla Ice. The price is $12 for adults and $6 for children. Call 379-5119.

  • Miami Lakes' 11th Annual Mother's Day Arts, Crafts and Gifts Show is from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday and Sunday. There will be more than 130 exhibits and free entertainment along Main Street just off Northwest 67th Avenue. Call 817-4198.

  • Spa of Tranquility offers two hours of relaxation for $90. Call 864-6010.

  • APS Training Centers offers computer educational packages for Mother's Day. Call 556-3040.

  • Husta Aviation will take moms on a plane ride for $80. Call 687-8410.
