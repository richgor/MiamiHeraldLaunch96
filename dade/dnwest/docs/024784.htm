Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

THE BUD LIGHT MOUNTAIN BIKE FESTIVAL CONTINUES THROUGH TODAY AT OLETA RIVER STATE RECREATION AREA, 3400 NE 163RD ST., NORTH MIAMI. ENTRY IS $3.25 PER CAR.

Registration for races opens at 8 a.m., and the first race, for first-timers, starts at 9 a.m. Other races are for beginners (9:30 a.m.), intermediates (10:30 a.m.), kids (11 a.m.), fitness seekers (12:30 p.m.) and experts (2 p.m.).

A party will follow at Shooters restaurant, beginning at 3 p.m.
