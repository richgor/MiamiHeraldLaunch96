Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

TOPS IN SURVEY

Here are the top three cars and trucks by category in J.D. Power and Associates' survey of more than 40,000 owners of 1996 cars and trucks in the first three months of ownership.
CARS

Compact Cars: Subaru Impreza, Mazda Protege, Toyota Tercel
Entry Midsize Car (Under $20,000): Nissan Altima, Honda Accord, Chevrolet Lumina
Premium Midsize Car (Over $20,000): Oldsmobile 88; Toyota Camry; Nissan Maxima
Sporty Car: Toyota Paseo; Acura Integra; Mazda Miata
Entry Luxury Car (Under $35,000): Lexus ES300; Mazda Millenia; Infiniti J30
Premium Luxury Car (Over $35,000): Lexus SC300-400; Lexus LS400; Infiniti Q45
LIGHT TRUCKS

Compact Pickup: Toyota Tacoma; Ford Ranger; Mazda B-series
Full-size Pickup: Chevrolet C-K; Toyota T100; GMC Sierra
Minivan: Toyota Previa; Mercury Villager; Chevrolet Lumina APV
Compact Sport Utility Vehicle: Ford Explorer; Geo Tracker; Jeep Grand Cherokee
Full-size Sport Utility Vehicle: Toyota Landcruiser; Chevrolet Suburban; GMC Yukon
