Published Thursday, May 9, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

DOMINICANS LIVING IN SOUTH FLORIDA WILL HAVE TO RETURN TO THE DOMINICAN REPUBLIC IF THEY WANT TO PARTICIPATE IN THE COUNTRY'S MAY 16 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION.

``There are no absentee ballots,'' said Nelcida Chakoff, executive director of the Dominican-American Chamber of Commerce. ``If you want to vote, you have to go there and vote. And lots of people are going; it's almost impossible to find [an airline] seat.''
