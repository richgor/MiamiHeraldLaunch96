CLIMATE: WEATHER CAN VARY DRAMATICALLY EVEN WITHIN SEASONS. MAY AND JUNE USUALLY ARE DRIER THAN JULY AND AUGUST. DAYLIGHT HOURS ARE LONG IN MIDSUMMER. WINTER CAN BE CHILLY AND WET.

Nearby attractions: Both cities are strategic starting points for exploring the highlands and hinterlands. At least 25 castles are within an easy half-day drive of Glasgow and Edinburgh.

Information: Edinburgh Information Centre, 3 Princes St., Edinburgh EH2 2QP, Scotland, United Kingdom; (011) (44) (131) 557 1700. Greater Glasgow Tourist Board & Convention Bureau, 35 St. Vincent Pl., Glasgow G1 2ER, Scotland, United Kingdom; (011) (44) (141) 204 4400. British Tourist Authority, 551 Fifth Ave., New York, N.Y. 10176-0799. Or call (800) GO 2 BRITAIN and request a copy of a 76-page, full-color brochure on Scotland.
