Published Monday, April 29, 1996, in the Miami Herald.


FLORIDA 5B

Lotto jackpot now $17 million

TALLAHASSEE -- (AP) -- The Florida Lotto rolled over to $17 million this weekend after no one picked all six numbers, lottery officials said Sunday.

Also, the first five Lottomobile III winners were announced this weekend on the lottery show Flamingo Fortune.

The winners of the red Cadillac Sevilles were Charles Butruch of Lake Worth; Alda Gonzalez of Miami; Mark Mathiasen of Miramar; John Moore of Ocala, and D. Stewart of Boca Raton. Five winners of the cars will be announced each Saturday until all 25 winners are known.

Even though no player matched all six numbers, there were more than 176,000 winners of smaller prizes:

  • 171 five-of-six winners get $1,740.50.

  • 8,875 four-of-six winners get $80.50.

  • 167,453 three-of-six winners receive $5.50.
