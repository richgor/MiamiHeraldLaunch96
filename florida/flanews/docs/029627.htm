Published Monday, April 29, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

PINES STRUTS ITS CIVIC PRIDE

MARSHA HALPER / Herald Staff
Santiago Contreras, 17, of Plantation, front, and Jerry Fortner, 16, of Pembroke Pines, dance their way down Pines Boulevard during a parade celebrating Pembroke Pines' 36th birthday. The duo, students at Nee's Kung Fu in Pembroke Pines, were one of 90 entrants in the Saturday parade.
