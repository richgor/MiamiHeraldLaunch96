1 ticket worth $17 million

TALLAHASSEE -- (AP) -- Just one ticket matched all six numbers drawn in this week's Florida Lotto game to win the estimated $17 million jackpot, the state Department of the Lottery reported Sunday.

That sole jackpot-winning ticket was bought in the Osceola County community of St. Cloud.

The five winners of red Cadillac Sevilles in a second round of drawings were G.W. Gunn of Winter Haven; Jack Turney of Wesley Chapel; Marie Hagen of Dunedin; Therese Churchman of Flander, N.J.; and Donna Buggica of Tampa.

Five winners of the cars are being announced each Saturday until all 25 winners are known.

In Saturday's Lotto drawing, 402 tickets matching five of the correct numbers each were worth $1,033, while the 20,877 tickets with four winning numbers can be redeemed for $47.50 each.

Payoffs of $3.50 go to all of the 363,147 tickets that got three of the correct Lotto numbers.

The Lotto numbers drawn Saturday night were 49-7-12-24-10-22.
