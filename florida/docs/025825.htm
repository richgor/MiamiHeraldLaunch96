Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

On way to court, man hits cop

TAMPA -- (AP) -- Antonio Valiente Valdez Jr. was on his way to traffic court Tuesday to face a charge of failing to wear his glasses while driving. Only he didn't wear his glasses and ran into a crashed car.

The impact pinned a police officer inspecting the first accident between the door and car, police said. Officer James Caswell, 50, was sent to Memorial Hospital with a bruised hip.

Valdez, 21, was charged with careless driving and failing to wear corrective glasses.
