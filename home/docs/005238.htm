Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

MEETINGS, CLASSES

TODAY

Bonsai Society of Miami Annual Spring Exhibit and Sale. When: 9:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Where: Fairchild Tropical Garden, 10901 Old Cutler Rd. Admission: Adults $8, children under 13 free. Bonsai demonstrations 11 a.m. and 1 p.m.

MONDAY

Orchid Culture Classes. When: 7 to 9:30 p.m. Where: Miami Palmetto Adult & Community Education Center, 7460 SW 118th St., Room 503. Instructor Jill Sidran. Cost: $45 for 10 weeks. For information, call (305) 235-1360 from 2 to 10 p.m. Monday through Thursday, 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Friday.

TUESDAY

Bromeliad Society of South Florida. When: 7:30 p.m. Where: Fairchild Tropical Garden, 10901 Old Cutler Rd. Program: Connie Johnson and Nancy Steinmetz will discuss preparing plants for the upcoming show. Harold Songdahl and Coralee Leon will talk about their new book, The Art of South Florida Gardening.

Coral Gables Orchid Society. When: 7 p.m. miniclass: Making an Orchid Corsage by Anne Anderson, Daisy Farm. 7:30 p.m. main program: Tim Anderson, Daisy Farm, on Plant Clinic . . . The Doctor is In! Pesticides & Fertilizers. Where: Fairchild Tropical Garden, 10901 Old Cutler Rd.

SATURDAY

Butterfly Gardening Class. When: 10 a.m. Where: Butterfly World, 3600 W. Sample Rd., Coconut Creek. Cost: $10 per person, preregistration and prepayment required. The park's weekend/holiday gate admission of $1 (children 5 and under free) will be in effect. For information, call (954) 977-4434.

WEEKEND

Tropical Flowering Tree Society Annual Show & Sale. When: 9:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Saturday and May 12. Where: Fairchild Tropical Garden, 10901 Old Cutler Road. Program: sale of rare flowering trees, shrubs and vines, educational displays and information, walking tours and lectures.
