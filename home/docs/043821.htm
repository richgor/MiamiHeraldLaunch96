Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

By SYDNEY OMARR

Los Angeles Times Syndicate

IF TODAY IS YOUR BIRTHDAY: You are dynamic, creative, have talent for writing, exude sexual energy. Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius persons play fascinating roles in your life. You are sensual, stubborn, can be self-indulgent to point of having sweet tooth. Current cycle relates to business enterprise, marital status, possible addition to family. Travel in June blends with creative endeavors, romance, style, additions to wardrobe. September will be your most profitable, creative, romantic month of 1996.

ARIES (March 21-April 19): Emphasis on distance, higher education, spirituality, possibility of long journey. You'll be free of encumbrances not your own in the first place. Libra, another Aries figure in scenario.

TAURUS (April 20-May 20): New light shed on finances of one who was close to you. Accent independence, originality, willingness to make fresh start in new direction. Leo, Aquarius persons play dominant role.

GEMINI (May 21-June 20): Dispute exists between family member who wants to stay home and another who insists on journey. Follow intuitive intellect and your heart. Marital status figures prominently.

CANCER (June 21-July 22): Fitness report featured. Recall resolutions associated with diet, nutrition. Spiritual matters discussed. Social activities accelerate; interest in politics surfaces. Sagittarius involved.

LEO (July 23-Aug. 22): You'll test theory that opposites attract each other. Exciting clash of ideas occurs -- you'll be asked to mediate. Sexual energy enlivens debate. Taurus, Scorpio persons in featured roles.

VIRGO (Aug. 23-Sept. 22): Check Leo message. Emphasis on dialogue, clash of opinions, study of special written material. Lunar position emphasizes quality of goods, sale or purchase of property. Gemini plays role.

LIBRA (Sept. 23-Oct. 22): Music in the air. Relatives converge -- much could be off-key, but also fascinating. Emphasis also on lifestyle, beautifying surroundings, learning more about plants, flowers. Taurus involved.

SCORPIO (Oct. 23-Nov. 21): Don't be shy about expressing psychic feelings. You're on right track, could be beginning of winning streak. Lost article returned, Pisces figures prominently. Virgo also involved.

SAGITTARIUS (Nov. 22-Dec. 21): Much revolves around your actions, statements -- you could be talk of the town. Focus on time, deadline, pressure of intense relationship. Designate where action will take place.

CAPRICORN (Dec. 22-Jan. 19): Complete project. Let others know, ``I am going on from here and will succeed.'' Romance looms large, involves travel, education. Secret meeting lends spice, involves Aries, Libra persons.

AQUARIUS (Jan. 20-Feb. 18): Obtain hint from Capricorn message. Affirmative, it is time to break free, to take initiative, to act in independent manner. Love relationship receives stimulus, involves adventure of discovery.

PISCES (Feb. 19-March 20): Obtain added wisdom from Scorpio message. Answer to question: Choose the unorthodox, usual routine won't work. Government involved; forms are to be filled out. Aquarian plays role.
