Published Sunday, April 28, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Miami-Dade at Kendall offers ecology classes

The Environmental Center at Miami-Dade Community College's Kendall Campus offers many noncredit courses.

For registration and class location call (305) 237-2161 between 8 a.m. and 8 p.m. Monday to Thursday, and 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Friday.

Ecology For Kids on Saturdays:

  • Butterfly Gardening, June 15, 10 a.m.-1 p.m., $10.

  • Plants Are People, Too, June 22, 10 a.m.-1 p.m., $10.

  • Rain Forest Ecology, June 29, 10 a.m.-1 p.m., $10.

  • Why Knot!? June 8, 10 a.m.-1 p.m., $10.

  • Worm Farming at Its Finest, June 1, 10 a.m.-1 p.m., $10.

    Class for adults:

  • Floral Design, Fresh Floral Arrangement, May 7-June 11, 7-9:30 p.m., South Miami High School, $55 plus $11.50 lab fee.

  • Victorian Pressed Flowers, June 5, 7:15-9:45 p.m., $15.

  • Cooking With Herbs From Your Garden, May 30-June 6, 7:30-9:30 p.m., Hammocks Middle School, Cost: $25.

  • Landscape and Gardening Care and Maintenance of Your South Florida Landscape, May 11-June 1, 10 a.m.-noon, $45.

  • Composting Workshop, May 11, 9 a.m.-noon., $15.

  • Container Gardening, June 15-29, 10-noon, $45.

  • Fragrant, Herbal, Edible Landscape Plants, May 18, 10-noon, $15.

  • Landscape Your Home, May 2-June 27, plus two Saturday field trips, South Miami High School, $112.80.

  • Liven Up Your Garden, June 8, 10 a.m.-noon, $10.

  • Nursery Certification Training, May 2-July 18, 7-9:30 p.m., $117.24.

  • Organic Vegetable Gardening, May 5-June 16, $65.

  • Pet Care, Feathered Pets, June 23-30, 10 a.m.-noon., $20.

  • Reptiles and Amphibians as Pets, May 15-May 29, plus one Saturday field trip, 7:30-9:30 p.m., Palmetto High School, $65.
