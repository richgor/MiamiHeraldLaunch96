AFTER YOU HAVE DECIDED TO HIRE AN INTERIOR DESIGNER, WHAT DO YOU DO NEXT?

The American Society of Interior Designers suggests the following checklist:
DETERMINE YOUR NEEDS

  • What do you expect from the completed project?

  • For whom is the space to be designed?

  • What activities will it be used for?

  • What's your time frame and budget?

  • Do you have a particular design style in mind?
    SEARCHING FOR AN INTERIOR DESIGNER

  • Ask friends and/or colleagues for designer referrals.

  • In South Florida, contact the Designer on Call service at the Design Center of the Americas, (305) 920-8115, between 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. Monday through Friday. People can make an appointment to tour the design center with one of more than 50 designers involved in the program. There is no obligation to the designer you meet.
    THE INTERVIEW

  • Inquire about the designer's educational background and experience.

  • Review the designer's portfolio.

  • Visit an installation completed by the designer.

  • Confirm the scope of the services offered by the design firm.

  • Determine whether your time frame suits the designer.

  • Assess whether your personality and the designer's are compatible.

  • Discuss budget and method of payment.
    FEES AND COMPENSATION

    In your interview, you and your designer should agree to one of the following compensation methods:

  • Fixed or flat fee.

  • Hourly fee.

  • Cost-plus.

  • Percentage fee.

  • Retail basis.

  • Cost by square foot.
    CONTRACTS

    After your designer has submitted a contract or letter of agreement, you should check for the following:

  • Are time sequences specified?

  • Does the contract specify the method and amount of compensation?
    THE DESIGN PROCESS

  • Establish a clear sense of how the interior designer will work at each stage of the project.

  • Negotiate and agree on a budget, time frame and approval process.

  • Make sure you stay informed about the project as it is carried out.

  • Confirm final decisions regarding design, layout and selection of furniture.
