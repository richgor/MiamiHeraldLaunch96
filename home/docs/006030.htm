Published Sunday, April 28, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

By SYDNEY OMARR

Los Angeles Times Syndicate

IF TODAY IS YOUR BIRTHDAY: You are romantic. When you love, it is all or nothing. Though you have been disappointed, where you are concerned it is better to have loved and lost than never to have loved at all. Leo, Aquarius persons play memorable roles in your life. You are an original thinker, you break from tradition, you are loyal, sentimental, passionate. Current cycle relates to political, charitable activities, travel, publishing, fashion. May most memorable for you in 1996.

ARIES (March 21-April 19): At last -- house in order. Be sure others are dealing from top of deck. Emphasis on law and order. Moon position highlights fitness, employment, basic issues. Scorpio plays role.

TAURUS (April 20-May 20): Lunar position emphasizes style, creativity, sex appeal. Your Venus ruler shines -- you'll draw to your creative, dynamic, musical people. Critical Virgo recants, admits, ``You're vindicated.''

GEMINI (May 21-June 20): Study Taurus message for added wisdom. Scenario features luxury items, beauty, music, domestic adjustment involving marital status. Stick close to home if possible. Important news on the way.

CANCER (June 21-July 22): Cycle includes blend of Moon and Neptune -- express yourself in poetic style; let others know there is much more to you. Relative visits; expects royal treatment. Pisces, Virgo in picture.

LEO (July 23-Aug. 22): Sword of Damocles removed -- relief from financial burden. Emphasis on timing, responsibility, pressure of deadline. Love relationship intense, too hot not to cool down. Capricorn involved.

VIRGO (Aug. 23-Sept. 22): Cycle high, trust your own judgment, intuition. Highlight originality, daring, courage of convictions. Correspondence from overseas is precursor to possible journey. Aries plays dominant role.

LIBRA (Sept. 23-Oct. 22): You'll ask, ``Is this deja vu?'' Scenario features familiar places, responses, faces. Break free from preconceived notions, inertia. You'll learn more concerning secret arrangement.

SCORPIO (Oct. 23-Nov. 21): Cunning associate tries to put one over. Be alert for double-dealings. Give full play to intellectual curiosity. Refuse to be fall guy. Friend brings help at last minute.

SAGITTARIUS (Nov. 22-Dec. 21): Excellent for social gathering, communicating desires to friends, family. Vindication is yours -- continue to adhere to principles. Another Sagittarian is at your side. No need to falter.

CAPRICORN (Dec. 22-Jan. 19): Facts and figures revealed due to your persistence. Moon position highlights distance, language, idealism in romance. Critical essay helps straighten out affairs. Taurus involved.

AQUARIUS (Jan. 20-Feb. 18): Get accounting -- turns out you have money coming, pronto. Flirtation lends spice; you'll be invited to attend prestigious social affair. Sagittarian in picture. Submit written material.

PISCES (Feb. 19-March 20): Clash of ideas featured. Express self in dynamic, confident manner. Focus on art, music, literature, ability to beautify surroundings. Get estimate of personal possessions, including antiques.
