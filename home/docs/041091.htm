Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

NEW MODELS

30-story condominium rises near Sunny Isles

By KIMBERLY C. MOORE
Special to The Herald

Ocean One Condominium Corp. is building a 30-story, 300-unit tower between Sunny Isles and Golden Beach. Ocean One will have concierge service, valet parking, 24-hour security, a health club, sun deck, pool, two tennis courts and a 10,000-square-foot recreational facility.

The model apartment, with three bedrooms, 4 1/2 baths and 2,358 air-conditioned square feet, starts at $315,900. A two-bedroom, 2 1/2-bath unit with 1,435 air-conditioned square feet starts at $179,000.

The sales center is open daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Ocean One is at Collins Avenue and 193rd Street, just north of the William Lehman Causeway. Call (305) 682-2222.

MonteRey opens new section

Morrison Homes has opened a new section of MonteRey, a private, gated community in Northwest Dade.

Zero-lot-line single-family homes range from $94,990 to $106,990. A two-bedroom, two-bath model with 1,260 air-conditioned square feet sells for $94,990. A four-bedroom, two-bath home with 1,700 air-conditioned square feet has a price of $106,990.

In another section, single-family homes centered on slightly larger lots start at $109,990. With 1,400 air-conditioned square feet, a three-bedroom, two-bath model with a one-car garage sells for $109,990. A four-bedroom, two-bath plan with a two-car garage and 2,100 air-conditioned square feet costs $136,990.

To reach MonteRey, take the Palmetto Expressway (State Road 826) to the 57th Avenue north exit. The entrance to MonteRey is on Northwest 57th Avenue on the right, just past Miami Gardens Drive. The office is open daily from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Call (305) 621-3000.

Four models at SummerWind

Royal Palm Homes has opened SummerWind in Coral Springs. The community will have 75 single-family homes when it is completed. There are four models.

The Gardenia has three bedrooms, two baths and a two-car garage in 1,744 air-conditioned square feet. Prices start at $139,900. The two-story Orchid model has four bedrooms, three baths and a two-car garage in 2,076 air-conditioned square feet for $154,900.

The two-story Magnolia has three bedrooms, a loft and 2 1/2 baths in 2,115 air-conditioned square feet. It sells for $156,900. The Hibiscus has three bedrooms and a loft, 2 1/2 baths and a two-car garage. It has 2,211 air-conditioned square feet and starts at $158,900.

SummerWind is on the corner of Coral Ridge Drive and Atlantic Boulevard in Coral Springs. The models are open daily from 10 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Call (305) 344-0488.
