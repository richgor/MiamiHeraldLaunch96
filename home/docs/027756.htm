Published Sunday, April 28, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Pembroke Pines grand opening

Pasadena Estates at Pembroke Shores recently held its grand opening. Built by Pasadena Homes, the Pembroke Pines community has lots measuring a minimum of 80 feet by 115 feet. The community overlooks lakes and a golf course.

Five furnished models are open. Included are the Arlington, with four bedrooms, three baths and a two-car garage in 2,570 air-conditioned square feet, for a starting price of $225,000. The Berkshire has four bedrooms, three baths and a three-car garage in 2,821 air-conditioned square feet, and starts at $239,000.

The Castleton has four bedrooms, three baths and a three-car garage in 3,047 air-conditioned square feet. The price starts at $255,000. The Dorchester is a two-story home with five bedrooms, 3 1/2 baths and a three-car garage in 3,489 air-conditioned square feet. The Empress has four bedrooms, four baths, a media room and a sitting room, all in 3,711 air-conditioned square feet for $289,000.

To reach Pasadena Estates, take Interstate 75 to the Hollywood/ Pines Boulevard exit and head west one mile to Dykes Road. Turn left. The sales center is open from 10 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. daily. From Broward, call (954) 437-7300; in Dade, call (305) 949-6700.

Estate homes in Coral Springs

The Isles at Lake Coral Springs has two new floor plans. The gated, Coral Ridge community has estate homes on the water starting at $325,000, with optional memberships available at the Tennis Club and the Tournament Players Club at Eagle Trace.

The Barcelona model is being built by Joseph Dobos. It has four bedrooms, a den, 3 1/2 baths and a three-car garage. It has 3,100 air-conditioned square feet for $439,900. The Lasta is being built by Edko Development and has seven bedrooms, five baths, a loft and a three-car garage. It has 4,605 air-conditioned square feet for $795,000.

To reach The Isles, take Interstate 95 or Florida's Turnpike to the Atlantic Boulevard west exit and head to Coral Ridge Drive. Make a right. The sales center is open from 9 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. Monday through Friday; from 9:30 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. Saturday, and from 10 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. Sunday. Call (954) 341-1000.

W. Palm Beach homes along golf course

Sandhill Trace is a new neighborhood at the Ibis Golf and Country Club in West Palm Beach. The homes overlook the 15th and 17th holes of a Jack Nicklaus-designed golf course. Homes on lakes are also available.

The Doral has three bedrooms and three baths in 2,625 air-conditioned square feet and starts at $335,000. The Essex also has three bedrooms and three baths. With 2,747 air-conditioned square feet, the Essex starts at $350,000.

The country club includes two golf courses, a clubhouse and a bath and tennis club.

To reach Sandhill Trace, take I-95 to the Northlake Boulevard west exit and go six miles.

The sales center is open from 9 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. Monday through Saturday, and on Sunday from 10 a.m. until 5:30 p.m. Call (800) 741-4500.

Four-, five-bedroom homes in Miramar

The Adler Cos. is building 101 homes in phase one of Stratford Estates at Windsor Palms, Miramar.

The gated community has six floor plans, all with four or five bedrooms. The four-bedroom Auburn has three baths and 2,766 air-conditioned square feet. The price starts at $221,990. The Carlton starts at $231,990 for four bedrooms and three baths in 2,991 air-conditioned square feet. The Durham is a two-story model with 3,050 air-conditioned square feet. It has four bedrooms and 2 1/2 baths and starts at $236,990.

To reach the sales center, take I-75 to the Miramar Parkway east exit. Turn right onto Southwest 148th Avenue, another right onto Lakeside Drive, and the first right to the sales center, which is open 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. daily. Call (954) 438-9040.
