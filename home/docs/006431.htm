Published Sunday, May 12, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

IF TODAY IS YOUR BIRTHDAY: You are interested in so many things simultaneously that you scatter forces. You are versatile,

possess sense of the ridiculous, have innate knowledge of fashion, current events, members of opposite sex have been known to describe you as ``devastating.'' Geminis, Sagittarians play significant roles in your life. Current cycle coincides with lifestyle, where you live, domestic adjustment that includes martial status. November memorable. ARIES (March 21-April 19): You'll be ``consulted'' by family members and others on subjects varying from money to marriage. Go slow, meditate, define terms clearly. Pisces plays role.

TAURUS (April 20-May 20): Needed material obtained, promise of funding legitimate. Focus on organization, renewal of special relationship with one who means much to you. Cancer native figures in memorable scenario.

GEMINI (May 21-June 20): Roadblock removed, views vindicated, you'll hear these words, ``I never really doubted you.'' Focus on love, travel, publication, participation in humanitarian projects. Aries is in picture.

CANCER (June 21-July 22): Take new look at proposals, business and marriage. Emphasis on new start, ability to put forth your own program. Emphasize initiative, courage, pioneering spirit. Leo is in featured role.

LEO (July 23-Aug. 22): Study Taurus, Cancer messages for added wisdom. Follow hunch, adhere to unorthodox procedure. Surprise discovery featured, involves finances of partner, mate. Legal loophole dominates scenario.

VIRGO (Aug. 23-Sept. 22): You'll learn more about how ``other half'' lives. Maintain perspective, humor. You'll be dealing with temperamental, talented people who appreciate and love you. Sagittarian figures prominently.

LIBRA (Sept. 23-Oct. 22): Just one step away. Refine work methods, sharpen tools, refuse to be discouraged by nay-sayers. Focus on fitness, basic issues, winning tests of determination, faith. Scorpio plays role.

SCORPIO (Oct. 23-Nov. 21): Be sensitive to Libra message. Communicate via writing, teaching. Spotlight on style, panache, romance. Moon position highlights stirring of creative juices. Gemini, Virgo involved.

SAGITTARIUS (Nov. 22-Dec. 21): Many individuals vie for your attention -- be selective, constructively critical. Elevate self-esteem, don't suffer fools. Lunar position emphasizes security, home, end of negotiations.

CAPRICORN (Dec. 22-Jan. 19): Relative attempts to burden you with problems not your own. Be specific in stating, ``I sympathize but that is all, except for tea and empathy.'' Your own dilemmas resolved by looking behind scenes.

AQUARIUS (Jan. 20-Feb. 18): Pressure applies in connection with accounting procedures, payment of bills. Organize priorities, know once and for all you are loved despite controversy. Capricorn native states, ``You're marvelous.''

PISCES (Feb. 19-March 20): Moon in your sign coincides with discovery, personal magnetism, being at right place at crucial moment. Distance, language barriers overcome -- recognition of soul mate could be imminent.
