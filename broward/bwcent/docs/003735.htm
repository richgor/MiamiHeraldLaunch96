Country Address treasurer seeks volunteer accountant

Country Address Homeowners Association in Cooper City may be the first to go online. New Treasurer Tom Cann has been busy entering association financial data into the Quicken program for record-keeping. But he'd like to find a neighborhood volunteer with some accounting experience to help close out last year's books so the group can begin with a clean slate.

Association President Peter Liston doesn't give a phone number for residents to call; he offers an e-mail address. Reach Liston at Peter.ListoninternetMCI.com

DAVIE/COOPER CITY

  • Summertime Isles Homeowners Association is helping promote economics for its residents. The association has published a directory of businesses owned by Summertime residents, and distributed it with its newsletter.

  • The Broward Sheriff's Explorers will have a golf tournament to benefit the 5-year-old son of deputy Alan Barnes. Adam Barnes was seriously injured in a hit-and-run accident in 1993. The child has brain damage and uses a wheelchair. Registration is at 6:30 a.m. and tee-off is at 7:30 a.m. Saturday at the Bonaventure Golf Club, 200 Bonaventure Blvd. Cost is $125 per person. Call Dottie Carlson-Perrin at 321-4150.

  • Sunshine Ranches, a community of equine lovers, will have a seminar on hurricane preparation for large animals from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at Masters Academy, 13900 Griffin Rd., west of Davie, on May 18. For details, call Brian Reynolds at 434-3984.

  • The Southwest Broward Volunteer Fire and Rescue has provided emergency service to residents in Southwest Ranches and surrounding areas for years. Once housed in a large garage -- built by their own hands with donations -- volunteers have been parking their trucks and equipment at a member's home for more than a year. A fund drive has been established to erect a station, but so far only $230 has been received.

    If you would like to help, send your tax-deductible contribution to 5150 SW 145th Ave., Fort Lauderdale, Fla. 33330.

  • The Rock Creek Homeowners Association will meet at 7:30 p.m. Thursday at the association office, 11700 Stonebridge Pkwy. in Cooper City.

  • The Ivanhoe Civic Association will meet at 7 p.m. May 13 at Hawkes Bluff Elementary School, 5900 SW 160th Ave., near Davie.

  • The West Broward Community Council will meet at 7 p.m. May 13 at the West Broward Sheriff's Office, 2525 Arvida Pkwy. in Weston.

    PLANTATION

    Lauderdale West Community Association will have a golf club meeting at 10 a.m. today. On Saturday, the shuffleboard club will meet at 9 a.m., a bridge seminar will be from 10 to 11 a.m., and a free movie will be shown for residents at 8 p.m. On Sunday, the Singles Club will meet at 10 a.m. and the Socialettes will present entertainment at 8 p.m.

    It's party time at 7:30 p.m. May 18, featuring the Diplomate orchestra. Tickets are $4 for residents and $5 for guests.

    All activities are in the Lauderdale West Clubhouse, 1141 NW 85th Ave., Plantation.

  • Omega Condominiums will have a Showtime at 8:30 p.m. Saturday featuring singer Bill Lawrence and comedian Eddie Barton of the famous Barton Brothers. Admission is $5 for the event, which will be at the Omega Clubhouse, 7200 NW 17th St. For more information, call 791-4268, 792-0237 or 587-9863.

  • Plantation Country Club Estates Homeowners Association will meet at 7:30 p.m. Thursday at the Plantation Community Center, 5555 Palm Tree Rd. in Plantation.

  • Plantation Acres Homeowners Association will meet at 7:30 p.m. May 14 at the Plantation Acres Improvement District meeting hall, 1701 NW 112 Ave. in Plantation.
