STATION LANDSCAPING: Impatiens line an area near the Shell gas station sign in Plantation.

Here are the winners of the 13th annual PLANT awards:


  • Large multifamily dwellings, more than 51 units, older than 10 years: Plantation French Quarter Condominiums, 408 NW 70th Ave.

  • Multifamily dwellings, 50 units or less: Jacaranda Villas No. 2, 8931 N. New River Canal Rd.

  • Commercial establishments greater than 10,000 square feet: Nob Hill Pavilion, northeast corner of Sunrise Boulevard and Nob Hill Road.

  • Commercial establishments of 10,000 square feet or less: a tie between Plantation Shell, 7901 W. Broward Blvd., and Pebbles Restaurant, 1280 S. Pine Island Rd.

  • New commercial establishments open six months or less: Eckerd Drugs, 10241 W. Broward Blvd.

  • Office buildings larger than 10,000 square feet: Cornerstone, 1200 S. Pine Island Rd.

  • Office buildings open six months or less: Kemper II, 1600 SW 80th Ter.

  • Renovations to a business: Broward Mall, 8000 W. Broward Blvd.

  • Public buildings, schools: Plantation Park Elementary, 875 SW 54th Ave.

  • Shopping centers: The Fountains Shoppes of Distinction, 801 S. University Dr.

  • Best maintenance: Broward Mall/Urban Retail Properties Co., 8000 W. Broward Blvd.

  • Environmental Award, given for extraordinary environmental consciousness: Chris Griffiths and Karolynne Vanyo's residence on East Acre Drive.

  • The city also recognized the Plantation Junior Woman's Club and Judy Ackerman for their work in the beautification of Broward Boulevard, as well as plumbing inspector Tim Fallon and urban forester Chris Richter with the Green Thumb Award for the Development Building's vegetable garden.
