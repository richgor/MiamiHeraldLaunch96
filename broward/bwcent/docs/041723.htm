Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.


CW HOMETOWN TYPE

POLICE REPORT

COOPER CITY

Cars scratched: In three separate incidents, car owners returned to their vehicles to find right-side scratches.

  • The first incident happened April 4 at Pioneer Middle School, 5350 SW 90th Ave. Someone scratched the right side of a 1988 Dodge Ram Charger, causing about $800 in damage.

  • Between 1:25 and 2:35 p.m. April 19 at Cooper City Hall, 9090 SW 50th Pl., a man found scratches along the right side of his 1996 Toyota Camry. The damage was estimated at $300.

  • Then, between 10 p.m. April 19 and 12:10 a.m. April 20, someone scratched the right side of a 1995 Honda. The damage to the car, which was parked in the 5800 block of Southwest 112th Way, was estimated at $300.

    Water meter stolen: Someone stole a water meter from a home in the 13100 block of Majestic Way between 5 p.m. April 19 and 7:30 a.m. April 24. The meter was valued at $125.

    License plate lifted: A license plate and its decal were taken off a car between 8 a.m. April 21 and 8:30 a.m. April 22. The car, a 1987 Pontiac, was parked in the 11600 block of South Budd Drive. The cost to replace the plate and decal is $6.

    DAVIE

    Lawn mower stolen: A thief broke into a trailer in the 13400 block of Southwest Third Court between 7:50 and 8 a.m. April 20 and stole a lawn mower valued at $250.

    Car burglarized: A thief broke into an unlocked 1991 Nissan parked at Colonial Apartments, 3800 SW 60th Ave., between 11:30 a.m. April 25 and 11:45 a.m. April 26. A thief stole a CD player valued at $150 and a leather wallet valued at $20.

    Food stamps stolen: A thief broke into a 1992 Dodge parked at Lincoln Park Shops, 6870 Stirling Rd., between 3:10 and 3:25 p.m. April 26 and stole a purse containing $25 and $200 in food stamps.

    PEMBROKE PINES

    Accident: After being issued a traffic ticket, a woman put her car into reverse and drove into the police car parked behind her in the 1100 block of South University Drive about 6:25 a.m. April 26. No injuries were reported. The woman's car sustained about $600 in damage and the police car sustained about $800, police said.

    Car taken: A thief took a 1993 Honda Civic, valued at about $17,000, from the Pembroke Lakes Mall, 11401 Pines Blvd., between 12:55 and 10:10 p.m. April 26, the owner said.

    PLANTATION

    Home vandalized: A home in the 11800 block of Northwest 27th Street was vandalized sometime between January and April. Vandals broke windows in the home, spray painted the walls and kicked holes in the walls, causing $250 in damage.

    Car burglarized: A thief broke into a 1995 Dodge Caravan at the Plantation Fashion Mall, 321 N. University Dr., between 11:35 p.m. April 28 and 6:45 a.m. April 29 and stole a purse on the passenger seat.

    Home burglarized: A thief broke into a home in the 7600 block of Northwest Fifth Street between 8:30 a.m. and 10:30 p.m. April 26 and stole a CD player valued at $150 and a ring valued at $500.

    SUNRISE

    Sneakers stolen: A thief stole a pair of sneakers, valued at $84, from Sports Authority, 12801 W. Sunrise Blvd., between 7:30 and 8 p.m. April 30.

    Jewelry stolen: A thief broke into a home in the 5900 block of Northwest 15th Street between 8:30 a.m. April 29 and 7:30 a.m. April 30 and stole earrings valued at $1,000 and a watch valued at $1,500.

    Restaurant burglarized: A thief broke into Mr. Pizza, 10053 Sunset Strip, between 10:30 p.m. April 29 and 9 a.m. April 30 and stole a cash register containing $300.

    WESTON/BONAVENTURE

    Jewelry stolen: A thief stole jewelry from a home in the 400 block of Racquet Club Road between 10 a.m. April 23 and noon April 28. The jewelry was valued at $4,500.

    Car stolen: A 1980 Jaguar X56 was stolen from the Seminole Truck Stop, 4690 U.S. 27, between 8 and 8:30 p.m. April 22.

    Home vandalized: Vandals broke into a home in the 7100 block of Southwest 185th Way between 8 and 9 p.m. April 23 and cracked a porcelain tub with an unknown metal object. The vandals did $300 in damage.
