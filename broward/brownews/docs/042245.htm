Published Monday, May 6, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Man found shot to death

Fort Lauderdale police Sunday were investigating the murder of a man who was shot to death in his duplex apartment.

Alejandro Lopez, 43, of 516 SW 14th Ct., was found dead in his home just after 2 a.m.

Police think he was shot during the night, but have few leads in the case.

Anyone with information on the shooting can call detective John Curcio at 761-5529 or detective Jack King at 761-5544.
