ABOUT THE SCHOOL

Charles Flanagan High School, 12800 Taft St. in Pembroke Pines, will open in the fall to ninth-, 10th- and 11th-graders. Here are some facts about the new school:

  • Named after: Charles Flanagan, former mayor of Pembroke Pines.
  • Construction cost: $28.8 million.

  • Mascot: Falcon.

  • School colors: Hunter green, black and white.

  • Capacity: 2,850 students.

  • Size: Nine buildings on 46 acres.

  • Parking: 695 spaces, of which 493 are for students.
