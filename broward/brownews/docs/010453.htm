Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

THE THREE SCHOOLS OFFERING HALF-DAY KINDERGARTEN FOR THE 1996-97 SCHOOL YEAR WILL HAVE INFORMATIONAL MEETINGS FOR PARENTS AND BEGIN REGISTRATION ON TUESDAY.

Parents may request either the 8 a.m. to noon or the 12:30 to 4:30 p.m. kindergarten session, but requests will be honored on a space-available basis.

Here is what you need to know:

  • Transportation to and from school will be provided.

  • A child must be 5 by Sept. 1 to enroll in kindergarten.

  • To register, parents must go to the school with the child's birth certificate, two proofs of residence such as a voter's registration card or utility bill, certificate of immunization with dates of each immunization, proof of a medical examination within the past year and two emergency contact telephone numbers. If your child already has a Social Security number bring that, too.

  • There are three registration periods. The first begins Tuesday and runs through June 7. Parents who complete registration during that period will be notified by mail after June 13 of which session their child is scheduled to attend. The next registration periods are from June 10 to July 10 and July 11 to Aug. 9.
