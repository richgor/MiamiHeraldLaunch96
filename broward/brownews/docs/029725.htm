Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

WHAT THE CITY COMMISSION DID

In other business Tuesday, the Fort Lauderdale City Commission considered the following issues:

  • Communications tower: Voted 3-2 to approve a 125-foot tower to be erected at 790 SW 21st Ter. PCS Prime Co. is the applicant. Commissioners want to develop a uniform policy to regulate the construction of towers.

  • Summer camp: Approved spending $176,666 in federal Community Development Block Grant funds to
    host the 10-week Learn and Play summer recreation camp program for 500 underprivileged children. Elementary school students age 4 to 12 will be eligible.

  • Water pollution: Agreed to have a public meeting at 6 p.m. June 4 on the controversial issue of live-aboard boats and the source of pollution to the New River.
