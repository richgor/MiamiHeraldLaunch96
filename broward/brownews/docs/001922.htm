HERE'S A TEST TO HELP EVALUATE YOUR NUTRITIONAL HEALTH.

THE TEST

Check the statements that apply to you. Then, to determine your score, add up the points and consult the scale below.

  • I have an illness or condition that made me change the kind of food I eat. (2 points)

  • I eat fewer than two times a day. (3 points)

  • I don't eat fruits and vegetables every day. (1 point)

  • I don't have milk products every day. (1 point)

  • I have 2-3 or more drinks of beer, wine or liquor almost every day. (2 points)

  • I have problems with my teeth, mouth or throat that make it hard to eat or swallow. (2 points)

  • I eat alone most of the time. (1 point)

  • I take three or more kinds of pills a day. (1 point)

  • I'm not always physically able to shop, cook or feed myself. (2 points)

  • I have unintentionally lost or gained 10 pounds in the last six months. (2 points)

  • I don't have enough money to buy the food I need. (4 points)
    YOUR SCORE
  • 0-2: Good. Recheck score in six months.

  • 3-5: Moderate risk. Try to improve your eating habits and lifestyle. For help, call Broward Meals on Wheels, 731-8770. Check your score in three months.

  • 6 or more: High risk. Take this checklist with you the next time you visit your doctor, dietitian or other health professional. Ask for help to improve your nutritional health.
    Academy of Family Physicians, American Dietetic Association and National Council on the Aging
