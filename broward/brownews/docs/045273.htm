Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

THE BROWARD COUNTY COMMISSION ACTED TUESDAY ON THESE ISSUES:

  • Dog pound fees: Agreed to raise fees at its dog pound for services ranging from vicious-dog registration to cat trap rental. Most fees go up $1.

  • Rabies clinics: Agreed to set up low-cost rabies clinics where low-income residents can take their pets.
