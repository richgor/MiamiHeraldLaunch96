BROWARD AND THE BUDGET

Here are major Broward County projects in the state budget approved by Florida legislators late Friday in Tallahassee:

Northwest Federated Women's Club headquarters $900,000
North Broward Hospital District crisis treatment unit $700,000
Weston student community center $270,000
Elderly kosher meals programs $206,000
Lauderdale Lakes Alzheimer's Center $200,000
Alzheimer's care at Cantor Senior Center $100,000
Shared human services computer system $100,000
One additional county court judgeship $102,000

FAU/BCC twin towers in downtown Fort Lauderdale $11 million
FAU/FIU Comprehensive University Presence (CUP) $ 6 million
FAU SeaTech ocean engineering center in Dania $1.5 million
FAU student activities center at Fort Lauderdale   $1 million
BCC Davie campus renovations $1.8 million
BCC Pembroke Pines campus renovations $1.2 million
BCC Coconut Creek computer lab renovations $231,000
FAU Davie emergency phone system and plaza $200,000
Nova Southeastern University optometry program $260,000
Nova Southeastern speech pathology program $215,000
CULTURAL & ENVIRONMENTAL
Eight community park improvement grants $850,000
Broward County beach renourishment studies $449,000
West Broward Boulevard improvement program $200,000
Stranahan House historic preservation grant $120,000
Fort Lauderdale history exhibit $104,000
