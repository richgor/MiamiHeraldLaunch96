Published Wednesday, May 8, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Cops seek kin in man's slaying

Fort Lauderdale homicide detectives on Tuesday released pictures of two men wanted for questioning in the weekend shooting death of a Fort Lauderdale man.

Jose Antonio Lopez, 50, and his son, Jaime, 25, are wanted for questioning in connection with the death of Alejandro Lopez -- Jose's brother and Jaime's uncle, police said.

Alejandro Lopez, 43, was found dead early Sunday inside his home at 516 SW 14th Ct. in Fort Lauderdale. Police are looking for the father and son because they were at the residence at the time of the shooting, detective John King said.

Police said the pair travel back and forth between Broward and Dade. Both Lopezes drive Ford Mustangs.

Anyone with information is asked to call King at 761-5544.
