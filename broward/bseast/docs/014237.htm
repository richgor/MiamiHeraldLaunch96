SE HOMETOWN TYPE

PET OF THE WEEK

Want to adopt a pet? Here's one of the animals available at the Broward County Animal Care and Regulation Division shelter.

  • Pet: Dog

  • Name: Doxie

  • Breed: Chow mix

  • Age: 3

  • Animal History No.: 07362

    The animal-care division says Doxie is very friendly, affectionate and gentle. She gets along with other dogs and is housebroken. She would make an excellent pet for a family without young children or a single person.

    Doxie, who has been at the shelter since mid-February, is one of the many dogs and cats available at the division shelter, 1870 SW 39th St., Fort Lauderdale.

    If you are interested in adopting Doxie or any other pets, call the shelter at 359-1313.
