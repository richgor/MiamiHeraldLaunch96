Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Pet shelters set to host adoptathon

By Herald Staff

S
ix South Florida animal shelters, including two in Broward, will join more than 700 shelters in the United States and Canada in a Pet Adoptathon today to find owners for thousands of dogs and cats.

These two Broward shelters will participate in the program, which started Saturday:

  • Broward County Animal Care and Regulation Division. The division will have two sites: 1870 SW 39th St., Fort Lauderdale, from noon to 4 p.m. Sunday. The adoption fee is $50 for dogs and $50 for cats, payable in cash or money order. For more information, call the Fort Lauderdale shelter at 359-1313 or the Pompano shelter at 970-0130.

  • Humane Society of Broward County. The site is at 2070 Griffin Rd. in Dania from 11 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Sunday. The adoption fee is $60 for dogs and cats. For information, call 989-3977.

    Other South Florida sites are: Humane Society of Greater Miami, 2101 NW 95th St., Miami, (305) 696-0800, and 19151 S. Dixie Hwy., Cutler Ridge, (305) 252-3389; Metro-Dade Animal Care and Control, 7401 NW 74th St., Miami, (305) 884-1101; Palm Beach County Animal Care and Control, 7100 Belvedere Rd., West Palm Beach, (407) 233-1200, ext. 198; and Peggy Adams Animal Rescue League of the Palm Beaches, 3200 N. Military Trail, West Palm Beach, (407) 686-3663.
