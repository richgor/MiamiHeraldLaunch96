Published Sunday, May 5, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

Police Report

Home burglary: A thief entered a home in the 500 block of Southeast 12th Street between 2 p.m. April 9 and 1 a.m. April 10 and took camera equipment valued at $3,000, $520 in coins and a diamond stud earring valued at $3,820.

Gun taken: Someone took a Colt .45 handgun from a room at the Crossroads Motor Lodge, 2460 State Road 84, between 6:15 a.m. and 5:30 p.m. April 5 while its owner was out of the room.

Moped gone: A thief took an unsecured Puch moped valued at between $700 and $800 from behind a home in the 3100 block of West Hallandale Beach Boulevard about 8 p.m. April 12.

Truck gone: A thief took a 1984 Mazda B2000 pickup truck valued at $2,000 from the parking lot of Shop and Save, 3163 W. Hallandale Beach Blvd., about 11 p.m. April 13.

Home burglary: A thief broke into a home in the 4500 block of Southwest 38th Street sometime from April 5 to April 8 and took the following items: a television set valued at $159, a videocassette player valued at $259, a television set valued at $319, a stereo valued at $160, two record players valued at $150, a stereo valued at $150, a tape deck valued at $150, a compact disc player valued at $159, computer equipment valued at $1,300, a video game system valued at $199, and a video camera valued at $2,596.

Car stolen: Police arrested a man in the 5700 block of Pembroke Road for allegedly stealing a 1995 Oldsmobile Cutlass Ciera in North Dade. He was stopped at 4 p.m. April 8. Metro-Dade police arrested Danilo Leon, 31, of 17055 NW 78th Ct., North Dade.
