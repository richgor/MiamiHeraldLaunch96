GAD SHOSHAN / For The Herald
MIRAMAR NEWCOMER: Principal Janet Ward says she became an educator because she loves to see children learn. Her goal, she says, is `to provide a safe environment so that all students can learn to their maximum potential.'

Principal wants kids to have success

  • Name: Janet Ward.

  • New assignment: Fairway Elementary School, 7850 Fairway Blvd., Miramar.

  • Birthplace: Ocala, Fla.

  • Family: One son, who is in college.

  • Career: Started out teaching English to secondary school students in Ocala before moving to Broward in 1973. In Broward, she worked at Deerfield Beach Middle School and Stranahan and Cooper City high schools, where she taught English. After working briefly as a guidance counselor at Coral Springs High School, she was an assistant principal at Piper High School for about 11 years. Afterward, Ward taught elementary education certification classes at Nova Southeastern University for about seven years. After moving back to Ocala for a year, where she served as an assistant principal in the Marion County public school system, Ward returned to Broward, where she was hired last year as principal at Nova Dwight D. Eisenhower Elementary School in Davie.

    Education: Bachelor's degree in English from Bethune-Cookman College, Daytona Beach. Master's degree in guidance and counseling from Nova Southeastern University. Educational specialist degree in administration and supervision from Nova. Doctorate in educational leadership from Nova.

    Subjects taught: English and college classes for elementary certification.

    Reasons for becoming an educator: ``I love children, and I love to teach them and see them learn.''

    Philosophy on education: ``To provide a safe environment so that all students can learn to their maximum potential.''

    Goal for Fairway: ``To provide a safe school that would have the capacity to reform so that we can meet the demands of society so students can be successful and live successful lives in the 21st Century.''
    -- JACQUELINE CHARLES
