Published Tuesday, May 7, 1996, in the Miami Herald.

POSITION: HEAD MEDIA SPECIALIST FOR HIGH SCHOOL EEE, 12800 TAFT ST., PEMBROKE PINES.

Education: Bachelor's degree in political science from Boston University. Teaching certificate from Florida Atlantic University. Working toward a master's degree in educational media at Nova Southeastern University.

Career: Worked in family travel business for several years before making a career switch to education. Arrived in Broward in 1988 to teach social studies at Plantation High School. After a year there, moved on to the newly built Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School in Parkland, where he taught social studies for five years. His last year at Douglas was spent as one of three media specialists in the school's media center.

Age: 42.

Birthplace: Marquette, Mich.

Family: Married with two children, ages 2 and 11.


DOUG PEARCE
