UH, THAT WOULD BE
MR. GOOFY TO YOU

Phillies minor league coach Larry Andersen on rookie starter Mike Grace: `Let's just say that if Disneyland is ever looking for someone to wear the Goofy costume, I've got the perfect candidate.' The perfect 5-0 candidate, that is.

BUNNY mug

ENOUGH ABOUT THAT
STUPID BUNNY

The Twins managed to score 10 runs -- and see 53 pitches -- in the fifth inning Tuesday against Kansas City before they made an out. `We were like the Energizer bunny,' said C Greg Myers. `We just kept going and going.'

HARRIS mug

IT'S MORE LIKE
MISSION IMPOSSIBLE

Joe Posnanski of the Cincinnati Post on the Reds' lousy relief pitchers: `Poor Ray Knight is in the dugout looking like Ed Harris in Apollo 13, desperately trying to figure out some miraculous way to get his Reds home.'

ALEXANDER mug

YES, YOU'RE DEFINITELY
OUT IN LEFT FIELD

Orioles infielder Manny Alexander walked into the clubhouse Thursday, saw a `7' by his name -- the designation for left field -- and asked what it meant. `It means you're pitching in the seventh inning,' 3B B.J. Surhoff said.
